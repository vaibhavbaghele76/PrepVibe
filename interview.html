  1. Why do you want to work for your company?
  2. Where do you see yourself in next 5 years?
  3. Why should we hire you?
  4. What are your career goals?
  5. How do you plan to achieve them?

Academic Background

  1. Tell me about your academic background.
  2. Have you done any internships or projects?Describe it.
  3. Which skills make you suitable for this job?
  4. How do you keep yourself updated with new technology?
  5. What was your favorite subject in college and why?

Company and Job Role

  1. What do you know about our company?
  2. Why do you want this position?
  3. Are you open to relocation?
  4. What is your expected salary?
  5. Are you comfortable with night shifts or flexible timings?

General

  1. What are your hobbies and interests?
  2. Who inspire you and why?
  3. How do you define success?
  4. What was your biggest failure and what did you learn from it?
  5. Do you prefer working in a team or individually?

Closing Question

  1. Do you have any question for us?
  2. If selected,when can you join to our company?
  3. Are you applying to other companies as well?
  4. Any final message you'd like to share?
  5. How do you plan to achieve them?
